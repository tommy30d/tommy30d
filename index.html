Palmerstown Set Dancing

Friday Night's 8:45pm till 10:30pm

Admission €5 ... tea/cake incl.


Hello and welcome to the Palmerstown set dance group.

We are a local, friendly group, who dance in the parish hall most
Friday night's from 8:45 - 10:30pm.

The hall has a nice wooden floor, with plenty of bounce.

Experienced &beginners of teenage years &ups are most welcome.
Tuition &instructions can be provided.

Bring a partner or come on your own.
Everyone will be partnered up in groups of 8 to form sets.

We break for tea &chat about 10pm &finish off with a familiar set.

Feel free to come along, join in the fun.
